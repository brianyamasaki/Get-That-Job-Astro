Common Things to Include
- [Contact Information](/en/resume/tips/contact-information)
- Work Experiences
- Education
- Volunteer Experiences
- Useful Skills
- Personal Projects, Hobbies and Clubs
- Leadership Experiences
- [A Goal or summary paragraph is optional](/en/resume/tips/goal-is-optional.html)


# Stuff to Leave Out of a Resume

- [Dates are not recommended](/en/resume/tips/dates-not-recommended.html)
- [Your address is not necessary in the contact information](/en/resume/tips/address-not-recommended.html)
- [Don't use icons or graphics for important text](/en/resume/tips/no-icons-or-graphics.html)
